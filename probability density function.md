>$$f(h) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \sigma^2}} \, e^{-\frac{(h - \mu)^2}{2 \sigma^2}}$$
We can now find $P(160<Y<170)$ by integrating the PDF of $Y$ from 160 to 170: 
$$P(160<Y<170) = \int\limits_{160}^{170} f(h)dh$$
